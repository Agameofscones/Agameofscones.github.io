New Varadero

Released: 2021
Game: Colonial Marines 13

Designed for low server population (40-90 players), on New Varadero the Marines must land and reclaim a USCM refueling depot that the Aliens have overrun after a Yautja ship was uncovered during a routine dig.

Based on the original Ice Colony V2, New Varadero is a faithful recreation of the classic CM13 map that brings new detail assets, style, and mechanics to the fold. Don't forget to pack some tackle, because this is the first map to feature the fishing mechanic!

Sorokyne Strata

Released: 2019
Game: Colonial Marines 13

Sorokyne Research Outpost, Population ~71, hides two secrets. The jungle beneath the frigid surface, and the Aliens inside that have broken loose. Marines must land on the exterior and battle through the snow fields, then onto the outpost's external deck, through the mega structure proper, and then break into the jungle interior and take the fight to the heart of the hive.

Sorokyne Strata was designed for 110-210 players and introduced new platform mechanics, handrails (and new barricade types!), a dynamic weather system, and temperature controlled areas. You can ditch the snow gear once you get inside. All with a commitment to realism; there are enough beds, dorms, and canteens for every official employee.

Fiorina Science Annex

Released: 2019
Game: Colonial Marines 13

Detached from the rest of the Fiorina Prison Complex, the dastardly experiments of the Company are revealed. Originally starting as an overhaul of the original Fiorina Prison Station map for CM13, it quickly grew into its own project. Besides mapping the entire thing on my own, I also sprited and animated a new set of hatch style doors to match the existing tileset. I also sprited and implemented standardized lore appropriate prisoner and guard clothing sets, including weapons and other implements.

Core Province

Released: 2016
Game: Half-Life 2: Episode 2
Download: Here

A proper propper experiment. Utilizing the Propper modeling utility for Source 1, this project focused on minimalist detailing using exclusively developer textures provided by HL2. Contrasted against foliage and natural textures, the player must fight through a series of enemies before extinguishing the flame of the core province, thus ending the level. Made for the Run Think Shoot Live HardVille mapping challenge. More of a visual experiment than one of gameplay. I wouldn't recommend playing it unless you like suitzoom sniping.

Kutjevo Refinery

Released: 2019
Game: Colonial Marines 13

On the planet LV-686 in the Dykuma System, Kutjevo Refinery bottles water (and processes other raw materials) for the Company. Though recently the wrong cargo container got shipped, and now there's a full blown infestation. Landing on the outskirts of the colony, the Colonial Marines must fight to the center of the caves and clear out the bugs. I sprited the walls (rock and metal), as well as the floortiles along with a myriad of other props for this map. I also introduced thermal tarp mechanics and button to blast door systems to change what combat lanes are open to which side depending on player interaction.

Released: 2016

Download: Here

Get burnt by a glass lake. Get jumped by Combine. Save like, 2 rebels. It's Glass Liberation! An entry into the LiberationVille Run Think Shoot Live challenge. Shiva's Snowball

Released: 2020
Game: Colonial Marines 13

A complete overhaul of a CM13 legacy map Ice Colony (v2). The original map was split into two Z levels, a topside and underground. This map is sister to New Varadero, that being the underground portion rebranded and remapped, and this being the topside re-imagined and redetailed. I sprited new walls (ice and fabricated), a whole boatload of props and other ground greebles to make this map visually pop.

Reshetka

Released: 2016
Game: Half-Life 2: Episode 2
Download: Here

Lost deep beneath City 17, the player must navigate hazards, mostly man-made, and mostly caused by maintenance negligence in a combat-less Half-Life 2 experience. This was the first project of mine that included heavy use of I/O scripting to build puzzles, traps, and dynamic lighting for the player to engage with. Developed for the Run Think Shoot Live TrapVille challenge in 2016.

Osmotr

Released: 2016
Game: Half-Life 2: Episode 2
Download: Here

The player is on a train car and must navigate through two trainstations in order to escape the underground. However between them and their goal are numerous Combine, Zombine, and other hazards. Themed after the Metro games, this was an experiment in advanced I/O scripting, including a stealth segment with detection cones and other things like a working train car. Frankly, the combat section is pretty awful and I can't recommend playing it without having buddha mode enabled. Young me was NOT good at encounter design.

Blade Symphony Adjustments

Released: 2018
Game: Blade Symphony

On contract with Puny Human I made various technical fixes and optimization passes on all of their Arena maps that shipped with the game in preparation for Harmonious Prelude. I also resized every combat arena to a single standard after collecting community feedback on the ideal combat space.

Images are of the map (Monastery) that required the most adjustment. Each arena required significant modification to meet the new standard, having previously been far too small.

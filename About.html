Hi my name is Gwydion W. Brain.

I make levels, maps, experience spaces, whatever you want to call 'em. Sometimes I make other stuff (like games, source art, irish tenor banjo sets), but mostly levels.

I've been making maps and levels since I was kid. It's a hard call between Starcraft 1 and Glider Pro, but I think John Calhoun's paper airplane classic is where I got my start. Maxis's Sim City 2000 certainly didn't help, but alas here we are. If there's one thing I find myself always returning to, it's making spaces for others.

There just ain't nothin' like watching someone get gibbed in courtyard geometry you put together.
